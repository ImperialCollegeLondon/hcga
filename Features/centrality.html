The basic centrality module¶

Centralities class.

class hcga.features.centralities_basic.CentralitiesBasic(graph=None)[source]¶

Centralities class.

Uses networkx, see ‘https://networkx.github.io/documentation/stable/reference/ algorithms/centrality.html`

Here we implement: Degree Centrality Eigenvector Centrality 1 2 Closeness Centrality 3 4 Betweenness Centrality 5 6 7 8 Harmonic Centrality 9
